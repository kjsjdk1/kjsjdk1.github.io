About Me

I am a physician-scientist and Professor of Otorhinolaryngology at Jeonbuk National University Medical School. I also serve as Chief Professor of Medical Informatics. My research focuses on sinonasal diseases, clinical outcomes research using large-scale databases, and the application of machine learning in medicine. With over 110 SCI-indexed publications and an H-index of 18, I am committed to advancing medical knowledge through innovative research methodologies.

Research Interests

Sinonasal Diseases

Chronic rhinosinusitis, nasal polyps, fungal sinusitis, and inverted papilloma

Head & Neck Surgery

Endoscopic sinus surgery, skull base surgery, and minimally invasive techniques

Clinical Outcomes Research

Large-scale database studies, meta-analyses, and systematic reviews

Allergy & Immunology

Allergic rhinitis, asthma, and immunological mechanisms in respiratory diseases

COVID-19 Research

Impact of COVID-19 on respiratory diseases and vaccination outcomes

Medical Informatics

Machine learning, AI-based diagnostic tools, predictive models, and big data analytics in healthcare
